SAN

Self Aware Network

Part I
Future

SAN is an experiment about the future of architecture.

A construction is represented by a network of nodes.

Networks are aware of themselves, the way we are aware of our body.

Networks understand the environment. They sense.

Networks communicate with their environment, visually and by audio.

Networks modify their environment by self-reassembly.

The network

The network is a construction that consists of nodes and edges. The tetrahedral symmetry of the nodes allows construction of the hardest structures known, the diamond structure. (SP³ hybridization)
